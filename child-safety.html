Child Safety Standards

Last updated: February 27, 2026

Gamarjoba Group LLC ("Gamarjoba", "we", "us") is committed to maintaining a safe platform and has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) content or behavior of any kind.

Age Requirement

Gamarjoba is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly allow anyone under the age of 18 to create an account or use our services. Users must provide their date of birth during registration, and accounts identified as belonging to minors are removed.

Content Moderation

We employ automated and manual content moderation measures to detect and prevent the sharing of inappropriate content on our platform, including:

  • Automated media scanning using industry-standard moderation tools to detect nudity and inappropriate content before publication
  • All user-uploaded photos and videos are reviewed before being made visible to other users
  • Content that violates our policies is immediately removed

Reporting

Users can report other users and content directly within the app. All reports are reviewed promptly. We encourage anyone who encounters potential CSAE content or behavior to report it immediately.

You can also report child safety concerns directly to us at: info@gamarjobadotcom.com

Blocking

Users have the ability to block any other user at any time, which prevents all further interaction between them.

Cooperation with Authorities

Gamarjoba complies with all applicable child safety laws and regulations. We report any identified CSAE content to the relevant regional and national authorities, including the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) where applicable.

Account Enforcement

Accounts found to be involved in any form of child exploitation are immediately and permanently banned. We reserve the right to report such accounts and all associated data to law enforcement.
