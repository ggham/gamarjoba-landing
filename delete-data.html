Delete Your Data

How to delete your account and data

You can delete your account and all associated data directly from the Gamarjoba app:

  1. Open the Gamarjoba app
  2. Go to your Profile tab
  3. Tap the Settings icon
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete Profile
  5. Confirm the deletion

What gets deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove all of your data, including:

  • Your profile information (name, birthday, gender, bio, email)
  • All photos, videos, and stories you uploaded
  • All chat messages you sent
  • Your location data
  • Your contacts and connection requests
  • Your notifications and device registrations
  • Any listings you created and their media
  • Your verification data and selfie

Timeframe

Account deletion is performed immediately. Any residual copies in encrypted backups are automatically purged within 30 days.

Can't access the app?

If you are unable to access the app and wish to delete your data, please contact us at info@gamarjobadotcom.com with the phone number or email associated with your account.
