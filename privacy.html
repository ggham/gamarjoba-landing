Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

Gamarjoba ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Gamarjoba mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Phone number, name, date of birth, gender, and profile photo.
  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect your location to show nearby users and events.
  • Media: Photos and videos you upload to share with other users.
  • Chat Messages: Messages you send and receive through the app.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and push notification tokens.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's core features (nearby discovery, chat, events).
  • To send you push notifications about messages, friend requests, and events.
  • To verify your identity through our biometric verification process.
  • To moderate content and ensure community safety.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. Media files are stored using encrypted cloud storage. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited data with:

  • Cloud service providers for hosting and storage.
  • Identity verification services for account verification.
  • Content moderation services for community safety.

Your Rights

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data through the app settings.
  • Disable location sharing at any time.
  • Control your profile visibility and notification preferences.
  • Request a copy of your data or account deletion by contacting us.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we will remove your personal data within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Gamarjoba is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at info@gamarjobadotcom.com.
